Your Turn To Die!

Your Turn to Die is about high school student, Sara Chidouin, who is forced into a death game with an assortment of other people who may share more in common than they know.

Sara Chidouin

Sara Chidouin is the protagonist of Your Turn to Die, and a second-year student at Sonobeno High School.

Joe Tazuna

He is a second-year high school student at Sonobeno High School and the best friend of Sara Chidouin.

Keiji Shinogi

Keiji Shinogi is a former police detective.

Kanna Kizuchi

Kanna Kizuchi is a second-year middle school student.

Q-taro Burgerberg

Q-taro Burgerberg is a baseball pitcher.

Sou Hiyori

He is a self-proclaimed job-hopper.

Reiko Yabusame

Reiko Yabusame is a 23-year-old singer-songwriter.

Nao Egokoro

Nao Egokoro is an art college student.

Kai Satou

Kai Satou is a self-proclaimed homemaker.

Gin Ibushi

Gin Ibushi is a 6th grader.

Kazumi Mishima

Kazumi Mishima is a high school teacher.

Gonbee Yamada

Gonbee Yamada is a man dressed in a prisoner outfit, with a mysterious and unsettling presence.
